### 1. Anthropic Overtakes OpenAI in Annual Revenue — $47B ARR vs. ~$25–33B
**Source:** [NateCue](https://www.natecue.com/en/news/anthropic-overtakes-openai-47b-arr/) · [AI Business Weekly](https://aibusinessweekly.net/p/anthropic-statistics) · [Sherwood News](https://sherwood.news/tech/anthropics-revenue-continues-to-surge-shooting-past-openai/) · [Zoom Bangla iNews](https://inews.zoombangla.com/anthropic-overtakes-openai-revenue-july-2026/)

Anthropic's annualized revenue run rate reached **$47 billion** in May 2026, surpassing OpenAI's estimated $25–33B ARR — a reversal that took just 17 months from a shared starting point of ~$1B in late 2024. Roughly 85% of Anthropic's revenue comes from enterprise API and developer customers; OpenAI draws approximately 85% from ChatGPT consumer subscriptions. Claude Code alone grew from $1B in annualized revenue at end-2025 to more than $2.5B by February 2026. Anthropic's post-money valuation following its $65B Series H now stands at **$965 billion**, edging past OpenAI's $852B to make Anthropic the most valuable AI company in the world.

**Why it matters:** This is the largest competitive shift in AI's commercial landscape to date. Anthropic's enterprise-first model has now definitively outpaced OpenAI's consumer-driven approach on revenue — a signal that the B2B agentic tier may be the bigger near-term prize than the consumer subscription market. It reshapes how investors and developers should think about platform risk and vendor concentration.

## New Framework

### 2. PyTorch 2.13 Lands — FlexAttention on Apple Silicon with ~12× Speedup
**Source:** [PyTorch Blog](https://pytorch.org/blog/pytorch-2-13-release-blog/) · [PyTorch GitHub Release](https://github.com/pytorch/pytorch/releases/tag/v2.13.0)

PyTorch 2.13 is out, with its headline feature being **FlexAttention on Apple Silicon (MPS)**: hand-written Metal kernels for sparse prefill and decode paths (including GQA and captured buffers) deliver up to **~12× speedup** over SDPA on sparse attention patterns. The release also adds a deterministic backward path (`compute_dq_write_order`) that replaces atomics with pre-computed write ordering for bit-for-bit reproducible gradients on CUDA without meaningful performance cost. A community Q&A is scheduled for July 22 at 11 a.m. PT.

**Why it matters:** FlexAttention's arrival on MPS means local Apple Silicon machines can now run custom attention variants — sparse, sliding-window, document-masked — at practical speeds. This significantly expands the set of researchers who can iterate on attention mechanisms without GPU server access. The deterministic gradient fix also closes a long-standing reproducibility gap for CUDA training runs.

### 3. Google Search Fully Transitions to Gemini 3.5 Flash AI Summaries — 93% of Sessions End Without a Click
**Source:** [Search Engine Journal](https://www.searchenginejournal.com/google-shares-first-ai-mode-usage-data-after-one-year/575443/) · [WrittenlyHub](https://www.writtenlyhub.com/news/google-ai-mode-1-billion-users-adoption-data) · [Thrive Agency](https://thriveagency.com/news/google-ai-mode-statistics-search-behavior-data/)

As of July 10, **Google Search results are entirely powered by Gemini 3.5 Flash-generated summaries**, replacing the traditional ten blue links across the board. Google's first AI Mode usage data (released July 11 to mark the one-year anniversary) reveals 1 billion monthly active users — the fastest Google product to reach that milestone — with the average query now 7.2 words (up from 4.0 for traditional search) and follow-up queries growing 40%+ per month in the U.S. A parallel Semrush study finds **93% of AI Mode sessions end without any click to an external site**.

**Why it matters:** The 93% zero-click figure represents an existential shift in web traffic distribution. Every publisher, SEO practitioner, and content business now operates in an environment where Google captures the value of their content directly in the search result. The 1B-user milestone also makes AI Mode the dominant consumer AI product globally by active users.

### 5. HHS Deploys ChatGPT Across All 50 States to Audit Federal Health Spending
**Source:** [On Healthcare Tech](https://www.onhealthcare.tech/p/hhs-goes-all-in-on-chatgpt-for-state) · [TechTimes](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/319108/20260625/hhs-deploys-chatgpt-medicaid-audits-no-error-rate-no-appeals-no-deadline.htm) · [Becker's Hospital Review](https://www.beckershospitalreview.com/legal-regulatory-issues/hhs-launches-ai-powered-audit-crackdown-on-states-grantees/)

The Department of Health and Human Services' **AERO** (Audit Enforcement and Risk Oversight) program — launched in May 2026 — is now fully operational across all 50 states, using ChatGPT and other LLMs to ingest and analyze Single Audit Act compliance records from every entity spending more than $1M/year in federal funds. The system targets chronic noncompliance, repeat deficiencies, and delinquent audit submissions, with findings that could result in loss of federal funding. Early analysis found audit deficiencies left unaddressed for five or more years and hundreds of grantees with overdue filings.

**Why it matters:** AERO is among the largest single deployments of commercial LLMs by a U.S. government agency and sets a precedent for AI-driven enforcement at federal scale. The lack of a disclosed human-review protocol or error-rate benchmark raises significant accountability questions that will likely shape how other agencies design LLM-assisted oversight programs.

### 6. OpenAI Deployment Company Acquires Northslope — Second Enterprise-AI Firm in Two Months
**Source:** [Axios](https://www.axios.com/2026/07/08/openai-deployment-company-northslope-acquisition) · [The Next Web](https://thenextweb.com/news/openai-northslope-acquisition-enterprise-ai-deployment) · [AI Weekly](https://aiweekly.co/alerts/openais-deployment-arm-buys-palantir-rooted-northslope)

OpenAI's **Deployment Company** — its enterprise-services subsidiary launched in May with $4B in committed capital — agreed to acquire **Northslope**, an applied-AI firm founded by Palantir veterans. This follows the earlier acquisition of AI deployment firm Tomoro and adds hundreds of "forward-deployed engineers" (FDEs) who embed within customer organizations to build AI systems around their actual operations. Deal terms were not disclosed.

**Why it matters:** OpenAI is structurally repositioning as a services company, not just a model provider — blurring the line between AI lab and implementation consultant. The Palantir-rooted founding team signals a deliberate move toward high-stakes government and enterprise verticals where Palantir built its business. If this strategy succeeds, it could lock in OpenAI's enterprise footprint in ways that pure API access never could.

### 7. Together AI Raises $800M Series C at $8.3B Valuation to Scale Open-Source Inference
**Source:** [TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/2026/07/01/neocloud-together-ai-raises-800m-leaps-to-8-3b-valuation/) · [Business Wire](https://www.businesswire.com/news/home/20260701243402/en/Together-AI-Raises-$800-Million-at-$8.3-Billion-Valuation-to-Make-Frontier-AI-Accessible-to-All) · [Together AI Blog](https://www.together.ai/blog/announcing-our-series-c)

Together AI closed an **$800M Series C** led by Aramco Ventures, with co-investors including NVIDIA, Vista Equity, General Catalyst, and Emergence Capital, valuing the company at **$8.3 billion**. Beyond the equity, investors committed over **500 MW of compute capacity** to be capitalized independently. The company reported $1.15B in annual bookings in its most recent quarter and plans to grow its GPU infrastructure footprint roughly 50× over five years.

**Why it matters:** Together AI is one of the clearest open-source alternatives to the major closed-API providers. The 500 MW compute commitment — equivalent to several large data centers — is the real story: it signals that open-weight inference at hyperscale is now attracting infrastructure capital previously reserved for closed-model players, which could structurally lower costs for developers who don't want lock-in to OpenAI or Anthropic.
