## New Models

### 1. Claude Sonnet 5 (Anthropic)
**Source:** [AIToolsRecap](https://aitoolsrecap.com/Blog/AINewsJuly2026.aspx) · [llm-stats.com](https://llm-stats.com/ai-news)

Anthropic launched Claude Sonnet 5 as the most capable Sonnet to date, approaching Opus 4.8 performance at an introductory price of $2/M input tokens (through August 31). **Why it matters:** Sets a new price-to-performance benchmark for frontier coding and agentic workflows, directly challenging GPT-tier pricing.

## New Agent Tools

### 5. Anthropic Claude Science Workbench
**Source:** [agentic.ai](https://agentic.ai/news) · [aitoolsrecap.com](https://aitoolsrecap.com/Blog/AINewsJuly2026.aspx)

Anthropic launched Claude Science alongside Sonnet 5—a workbench with 60+ preconfigured tools aimed at researchers, plus an internal drug discovery program targeting neglected diseases.

**Why it matters:** Signals Anthropic's push into scientific agentic applications beyond coding and chat.

---

### 6. Cloudflare Granular AI Bot Management
**Source:** [toolchase.com](https://toolchase.com/blog/ai-tools-news-2026/) · [agentic.ai](https://agentic.ai/news)

Cloudflare launched fine-grained controls letting site owners separately manage Search, Agent, and Training crawlers. New defaults block Agent and Training bots on ad-supported pages while permitting Search crawlers.

**Why it matters:** First major infrastructure-layer differentiation between agent traffic and standard web crawlers—significant for anyone building or deploying web agents at scale.
